The identifiers James Isaac Nyamweya have been used to differentiate me since birth.I had no say whatsover in their choosing.However they make a pathetic attempt at describing the symbiotic collection of cells that I am.The question then is,who am I?

I am :a Human being,an African,a Kenyan,a pacifist,an embracer of technology,a programmer.

I like sofware development.There's a euphoria in watching your creation come to life .I hope this site will be a fair representation of who I am.

A dreamer;I have no regrets being born in this era.The possibilities of us starting a colony on Mars or building Jurassic Park have never been more real.Exciting times are these.This millenium generation could so easily become the immortal generation then isn't it our right to go and tame the universe then.

In general, I hope to contribute to a world where we value skills and relationships over careers and money, where we know better than to trust the system, and where we're passionate about building and creating things in a self-motivated and self-directed way.
